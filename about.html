Who is He?

Dennis is an uncontrollable schoolboy who takes pride in causing chaos and mayhem to those around him due to his intolerance for rules and order. He is the bad boy of the school, The main recurring storyline throughout the years features his campaign of terror against a gang of "softies" (effeminate, well-behaved boys), particularly Walter the Softy. Walter finds himself in unfavourable circumstances on many occasions, although he sometimes gets the last laugh. Dennis is quite often considered the villian in the story. He would often prove himself to be selfish and greedy, tending to disregard his friends in favour of treasures. Dennis is often considered to be a loner, seeking no solace in anyone's company aside from his faithful pet dog Gnasher, however he does have a fairly stable friendship with Minnie the Minx and Roger the Dodger.

Characters

  • Dad - In the 2012 revamp, he was redrawn to look more like Dennis. This was explained in 2015 as Dad now being the grown-up version of the 1980s Dennis the Menace, making the original Dad the new Dennis' grandfather.
  • Mum
  • Bea - Dennis's little sister, born in issue 2931, dated 19 September 1998, Bea wears a stripy yellow and black vest, this is a pun on her name as the colours are the same as the colours on a Bumble bee.
  • Granny - is Dennis's and Bea's 80-year-old grandmother, In the late 1980s/early 1990s, she got her own strip Go, Granny, Go!
  • Denise - Dennis's cousin, who bears a striking resemblance to Beryl the Peril, although she also wears a red-and-black striped jumper. She generally managed to out-menace Dennis, showing considerable fortitude against the Softies, and at the local boxing club.
  • Gnasher - a black dog, He has extremely strong teeth that can leave teeth marks in seemingly anything and enjoys chasing postmen
  • Gnipper - Son of Gnasher
  • Rasher - He loves to eat swill and was rescued by Dennis. Rasher also has children: Oink, Snort, Grunt, Squeal, and Squeak.
  • Dasher - Dennis' pet spider
  • Dennis' friends - Curly (real name Crispin Lee Shepherd) and Pieface (real name Kevin Peter Shepherd)
