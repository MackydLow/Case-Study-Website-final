What is it?

DC Thomson is a media production company located in Dundee. DC Thomson publishes many newpapers and comics, and also owns the Aberdeen Journals Group (publishers of the Press and Journal). Although the company D.C. Thomson & Co. Ltd was founded in 1905 by David Couper Thomson to manage the newspaper side of the business, the roots of Thomson family and how they began their printing journey dates back to 1838 when William Thomson II commenced a business on union street - Dundee. Around the time of the companys prime in 2010. Over 100 million copies of magazines, comics and papers were being produced per year.
